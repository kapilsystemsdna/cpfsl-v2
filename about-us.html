About us

With a professional team headed by visionary leadership, our greatest recommendation lies in the fact that 100% of our clientele comprises businesses and professionals won over to our side strictly based on either word of mouth or previously satisfied clients. Corporate Planning is an independent financial services broker who can offer financial advice that is catered only for you, keeping in mind your current needs and future wishes.

Our team comprises of trained professionals with ample past experience which we aim to use for your benefit. With continued knowledge upgrades, we constantly keep acquainted with the best practice in business and current market trends to optimally service our varied clientele. At Corporate Planning, we believe in a mutual relationship of understanding that suits both, the client's needs, and our services.

We use a hands-on approach along with years of experience to resolve the emotional aspects of selling family companies - or buying into them. Consequently, they are well versed in finding solutions to those intangible or unvoiced issues that often arise.
