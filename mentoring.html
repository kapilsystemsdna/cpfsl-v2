Mentoring

For the past 3 years Paul has been an award-winning mentor on the Santander 'Breakthrough Women Business Leaders' Mentoring Programme powered by Moving Ahead.

This mentoring scheme is for the next generation of fast-growth female entrepreneurs. The programme matches women business leaders from across the UK with carefully selected mentors from a variety of industries and backgrounds, who will support their mentees as they consider the next stage of their business and professional growth.

Paul's mentees have enjoyed huge success on the programme and have benefited greatly from his wealth of experience. In 2020 Heather won the 'Most Improved Mentee' award and in 2021 Sammie won the special Santander Breakthrough Award for 'Personal and Business Transformation'. In addition, Paul was highly commended as one of the top 5 Most Inspirational Mentor's in 2020 and 2021.

Paul understands that for a business to succeed it is necessary to look beyond the balance sheet and consider other aspects of running a business including:
  • Personal wellbeing
  • Staffing needs
  • Location
  • Organisation skills
  • Networking
  • Prioritising workloads

Many clients come to Corporate Planning thinking they need finance, but what they really need is help with how to run and grow their business.

Paul is now offering his experience gained from his work with the Santander Breakthrough Women Business Leaders' Mentoring Programme to help all business leaders look 'beyond the balance sheet' and advance their businesses to the next level.
