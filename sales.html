Company Sales

Everyone at Corporate Planning knows that the process of selling a business can be a traumatic experience akin to divorce, adoption or bereavement. With this knowledge at the forefront of our minds we aim to make the process as seamless and smooth as possible. We use a hands-on approach along with years of experience to resolve the emotional aspects of selling family companies - or buying into them. Consequently, we are well versed in finding solutions to those intangible or unvoiced issues that often arise.

Corporate Planning operates on the niche concept of commercial match-making. We uses extensive client database to facilitate a union between acquisitive buyers and keen sellers and vice versa. Our counter-intuitive approach ensures that we find buyers and sellers that are on no-one else's radar. At Corporate Planning we keep clients wishes at the centre of all as approaches. we are confident in advising our clients for a joint venture or a merger rather than the original straight sale (or purchase) envisaged.

If you are thinking about exiting your business whether it be for retirement or simply wanting a new challenge, there are different options to consider:
  • Full exit
  • Partial exit
  • Management Buy Out (MBO)
  • Management Buy In (MBI)
  • Initial Public Offering (IPO)
  • Pass to family
  • Employee Ownership Trust (EOT)
  • Step down from office
